About Us

Welcome to Diarmuid, a pioneering initiative dedicated to empowering women of all ages through the innovative use of upcycled technology.

At Diarmuid, we believe in the transformative power of information and communication technology (ICT) and are committed to making it accessible to everyone, women who have been historically underrepresented in the tech industry.Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing comprehensive ICT education using repurposed computers and electronic scrap.

Our Impact

What we do

  • Technology: We collect and refurbish old computers and electronic scrap, transforming them into valuable learning tools. Our upcycling process is environmentally friendly and helps reduce e-waste.
  • ICT Education: Our curriculum is designed to cater to women of all ages and backgrounds. Whether you are a beginner looking to learn the basics or someone seeking advanced skills, we have courses tailored to meet your needs.
  • Community Building: We provide a supportive and inclusive environment where women can connect, collaborate, and grow. Our community-driven approach ensures that every participant feels valued and encouraged.
